IN MONO IWINYO PI CIK ANGWEN ME CWINY?
Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws?
Tye cik me lobo ma gwoko lobo maa ma makwo lye ni. Dok bene tye cik me cwinyma tye ka notte ikin in ki Lubanga meri.
There are natural laws that control the world in which we live. There are also spiritual laws which determine your relationship with God.
Cik me Acel
LAW ONE

Lubanga omaro in dok bene en tye ki yub maber oi kwo ma meri.
God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

Mar pa Lubanga
God’s Love

Pien Lubanga con omaro lobo kumeno, omiyo iye Wode acel keken, wek dano ducu ma ye en owek to, ento obed ki kwo ma pe tum.Jon 3:16
“God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life.” (John 3:16)

Yub pa Lubanga
God’s Plan

(Rwot Yecu owaco,)”An abino wek gunong kwo, gubed kwede madwong.”Jon 10:10
[Christ speaking] “I came that they might have life, and might have it abundantly” [that it might be full and meaningful]. (John 10:10)
Cik me Aryo
LAW TWO

Dano en labal dik opoke woko ki Lubanga, pi man en pe twero ngeyo dok winyo mar pa Lubanga ki yub pi kwone.
People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

Dano en labal
people are Sinful

Pien dano ducu gubak\lo pe gio I kom deyo pa Li\ubanga. Jo Roma 3:23
“All have sinned and fall short of God’s glorious standard.” (Romans 3:23)

Dano gicweyo me bedo larerm ki Lubanga. Ento en okwanyo yo ma mere cibedo larem ki Lubanga otur woko, Man aye gin ma Jiri lwongo ni bal. Man nyute
We were created to be friends with God; but we chose to go our own way, and friendship with God was broken. This is what the Bible calls sin. This is shown:
1.  Ikare ma dano ojemo ikom Lubanga
1.  when people rebel against God,
2.  Ikare ma en pe tamo pi Lubanga, ki
2.  when people do not care about God and
3.  Ka en pe kwo ma pe atir inyim Lubanga
3.  when people do not live up to God's perfect standard

Dano Opoke Woko
People are Separated

Pien mucara me bal en to (poke i cwiny ki Lubanga). Jo Roma 6:23
“The wages of sin is death” [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23)

Lubanga pe ki bal ento dano lubalo. Tye apokapoka madit ikin jo aryo man. Lacim megu nyuto kit ma dano tye ka teme kwede me oo bot Lubanga, ki me nongo kwo ma pe tum, ki teko ma mere: kwo ki kwo maber, bedo ladini, konyo dano mukene, ento tutine lal nono.
God is holy and people are sinful. There is a great separation between the two. The arrows show how people try to reach God and the abundant life through their own efforts: such as living a good life, being religious, helping others etc., but all their efforts fail.
The third law gives the only answer to this problem…
Cik me Adek
LAW THREE

Yecu Kricito aye yo ma dano oo kwede bot Luban-ga. En oto pi wan. Ki bot Yecu iromo ngeyo Lubanga dok iwinyo mar mere kacel ki yub ma en tye kwede pi kwo ma meri.
Jesus Christ is theONLY way people can reach God. He died for us. Through Him you can know God and experience His love and plan for your life.

En oto ka ocer ki I liyel.
He Died and Rose From the Dead

Pien Kricito bene yam onene ca pi bal wang, kicel ma otyeko ducu: en ngat ma kite atir oto po jo ma kitgi pe atir, wek omi ekelwa bot Lubanga…1 Petero 3:18
“For Christ died for sins once for all, the righteous for the unrighteous, to bring you to God”. (1Peter 3:18)

Obedo ka nen botgi tyen mapol pi moko ni etye makwo lacen ki I nge tone. Obedo ka nen botgi pi nino pyerangwen (Tic pa Lukwena 1:3)
“After his suffering, he showed himself to these men and gave many convincing proofs that he was alive. He appeared to them over a period of forty days and spoke about the kingdom of God.” (Acts 1:3)

En keken aye yo
He Is the Only Way

Yecu odokke iye ni, “An a yo, ki ada, ki kwo: Pe tye ngati mo ma bino bot Won, kono ki I en keken”Jon 14:6.
“Jesus said to him, ‘I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father, but through Me’.” (John 14:6)

Lubanga omwona woko apokapoka man ma tye I kin wa ki En ki cwalo Wode Yecu Kricito me too I kon yatarita u kakarwa.
God has bridged this separation between us and Himself by sending His Son Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place.
Ngeyo cik adek man pe twero konyo wa …
It is not enough just to know these three laws…
Cik me Angwen
LAW FOUR

Myero wan acel acel wajol Yecu Kricito calol Lalar ki Rwot; ci watwero ngeyo mar ki yub pa Lubanga pi kwowa.
We must individuallyRECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

Myero wajol Kricito
We Must Receive Christ through faith

Ento dano ducu ma ceng gujole, ma guye nyinge, omiyogi twero me doko litino pa Lubanga. Jon 1:12

“As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name” (John 1:12)

Pien pi kicane omiyo en olarowu pi niye. Man pe otimme pi tic mo ma win wutiyo; meno obedo mot pa Lubanga gire. Pe otimme pi ticwi mo, mumiyo ngat mo owek wakke pire. Jo Epeco 2:8-9.
“By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works, that no one should boast” (Ephesians 2:8,9).

Watwero jolo Kricito ki lwongo En i kwowa
We Receive Christ by inviting him into our lives

Pe romo me ye pwony pa Kricito keken. Jolo Kricito keken. Joko Kricito nyutu lokke bot Lubanga ki gin abalawa (tucu bal). Wageno Kricito me bino i kwowa ki me bwoto gin abalawa. Wawek En olok wa kit ma En mito wabed kwede.
[Christ is speaking] “Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if any one hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him.” (Revelation 3:20)

Itwero jolo Kricito Kombedi ni?
You Can Receive Christ Right Now

Peny En me bino i kwoni. Gen En me timo gin ma En ocicko. En tamo totwal gin ma ibigeno i cwinye pe i lok ma iloko. Onyo itwero lego kitman:
Ask Him to come into your life. Trust Him to do what He has promised. He is more concerned with what you mean in your heart than in the words you say. You may want to pray:

“Rwot Yecu, amito in. Ibedi ka loyo kwo ma mera ci abalo boti. Apwoyo in pi bwoto bal mera. Bin ikwo ma mera ci ilo kwona bene. Lok an abed kit dano ma in imito. Amen”
“Lord Jesus, I need You. I have been controlling my own life and have sinned against You. Thank You for forgiving my sins. Come into my life and take control of my life. Make me the kind of person You want me to be. Amen.”

Lega man tye ka nyuto miti ma tye i cwinyi?
Does this prayer show the desire of your heart?

Ka tye ka nyutu miti, legi lega man kombedini. ci Kricito bi bino ikwoni. kit ma En ociko.
If it does, pray this prayer right now, and Christ will come into your life, as He promised.

Kit me ngeyo ka Kricito tye i kwoni
How to Know That Christ Is in Your Life

Baibul ociko kwo ma pe tum bot dano ducu ma jolo Kricito.
The Bible Promises Eternal Life to all who receive christ

Lubana dong omiyowa kwo ma pe tum, dok kwo meno tye i Wode. Ngat ma tye ki Wod tye ki kwo, ngat ma pe ki kwo, ngat ma pe ki Wod pa Lubanga, pe tye ki kwo. Acoyo lok man botwu, wun jo muye nying Wod pa Lubanga, wek wunge ni wutye dong ki kwo ma pe tum 1Jon 5:11-13
“The witness is this, that God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son. He who has the Son has the life; he who does not have the Son of God does not have life. These things I have written to you who believe in the name of the Son of God, in order that you may know that you have eternal life” (1 John 5:11–13).

Pwo lbanga nino ducu pien kricito tye i kwoni. En owaco ni en pe bi weko in (Jo Ibru13:5). Itwero ngeyo ni Kricito kwo i komi doki nii i tye ki kwo ma pe tum pien En ociko kumenu.
Thank God often that Christ is in your life. He will never leave you (Hebrews 13:5). You can know that Christ lives in you and that you have eternal life because of His promise.
Tam kono?

Pe Igen tam ma meri
Do Not Depend on Feelings

Lakricitayo kwo ki niye i kom Lubanga ki gin ma En loko i Baibul. Gicike ilok pa Lubanga en aye twero ma mewa.
The Christian lives by believing God and what He says in in the Bible. The promise of God's Word is our authority.

Cal me gaar man tye ka nyuto kit ma ada me Baibul, niye ki Tam woto kacel i kwo pa Lakricitayo.
This drawing of a train shows how the Truth of the Bible, Faith and Feelings work together in your Christian life.
1.  Ada tye ki teko me terowa.
1. Truth has the power to lead us.
2.  Waribe ki teko pa ada ki niye.
2.  We are joined to the power of Truth by Faith.
3.  Kit ma wa woro ada, tamwa ma lubowa i yoo atir.
3.  As we obey the Truth, our feelings follow along in the right way.
4.  Wa geno Lubanga ki ada mere, pe tam ma mewa.
4.  We trust God and His Truth, not our Feelings

Kombedi kit ma dong ijolo Kricito
Now That You Have Received Christ

Ikare ma ijolo Kricito, jami mapol otime:
The moment that you received Christ by faith many things happened:
1.  Kricito obino i kwoni. Niyabo 3:20 ki Jo Kolocai 1:27
1. Christ came into your life. (Revelation 3:20 ; 
Colossians 1:27)
2.  Bal ma meri gibwoto woko. Jo Kolocai 1:14
2. Your sins were forgiven. (Colossians 1:14)
3.  Idoko latin pa Lubanga. Jon 1:12
3. You became a child of God. (John 1:12)
4.  Ibicako ngeyo yub pa Lubanga ki gin ma en mito pi kwo ma meri. Jon 10:10 ki 2Jo Korinto 5:14, 15, 17.
4. You will begin to experience God's love and purpose for your life (John 10:10 and II Corinthians 5:14,15,17).

Itwero tamo pi gin mo maber ma romo time boti ma loyo jolo Kricito?
Can you think of anything more wonderful that could happen to you than receiving Christ?

Imito pwoyo Lubanga i lega icaa man pi gin ma En otimo ikwo meri? Miyo pwoc bot Lubanga nyuto niyr meri. Walegu.
Would you like to thank God in prayer right now for what He has done for you? By thanking God, you demonstrate your faith.

Kit me dongo me Cwiny
Suggestions for Christian Growth

Macalo lakricitayo, mito i dong. Tam magu twero konyo in.
As a Christian we should grow. This outline will help you:
1.  Citi bot Lubanga ki lega nino ducu. Jon 15:7
Go to God in prayer daily. (John 15:7)
2.  Kwan lok pa Lubanga nino ducu. Tic pa Lukwena 17:11. Cak ki Jiri pa Jon.
Read God’s Word daily (Acts 17:11); begin with the Gospel of John.
3.  Winy cik pa Lubanga, kare ki kare. Jon 14:21
Obey God moment by moment. (John 14:21)
4.  Bed lacaden pa Kricito ki kwo wa ki lok ma meri. Matayo 4:19; Jon 15:8
Witness for Christ by your life and words. (Matthew 4:19; John 15:8)
5.  Gen Lubanga i kwo ni ducu. 1Peter 5:7
Trust God for every detail of your life. 
(1 Peter 5:7)
6.  Bed ki teko pa Lubanga, wek olo kwoni nino ducu ki caden. Jo Galatiya 5:16,17 ki Tic pa Lukwena 1:8
Have God control and empower your daily life and witness. (Galatians 5:16 & Acts 1:8)

Kanica pire tek
The church is important

Ka yen tye kacel, gi wang maber Ket dog yen acel i ka ma ngic ci mac ce cako too. I kit yoo acel ni, bi bedo tek boti me kwo kwo pa lakricitayo in keni.
When logs are together; they burn brightly. Put one aside on the cold ground and the fire will begin to go out.

Pien pire tek mada ni ka iribe ki jo mukene ma gujolo Kricito ma calo Rwot ki Lalar ma megi.
It is important that you should join other people who have received Jesus Christ as their Lord and Saviour.

Omyero iciti i kanica ka ma dano ye ni Jiri en ada, doki ka ma kricito giworo iye. Jo Ibru 10:25
You should go to a Church where people believe that the Bible is true and where Jesus Christ is obeyed. (Hebrews 10:25)
